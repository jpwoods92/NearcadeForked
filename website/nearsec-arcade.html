Nearcade Arcade

Nearcade Arcade is a decentralized, community-driven platform for discovering local multiplayer games hosted by players worldwide.

All sessions are hosted directly from the host's computer via high-speed WebRTC tunnels. Gameplay data flows peer-to-peer and is not routed through our servers.
